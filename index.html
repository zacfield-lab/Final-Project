Favorite Destinations

These are the places I love most. Each destination has a story, a local flavor, and a set of travel lessons I keep using.

  1. Amalfi Coast
    • Cliffside villages, lemon groves, and scenic coastal drives.
    • Fresh seafood and vibrant seaside sunsets.
  2. The Cotswolds
    • Quaint stone villages and rolling green hills.
    • Cozy tea rooms and historic country inns.
  3. Mont St Michel
    • Medieval abbey perched above a tidal island.
    • Unique tides and a timeless island village experience.

Why Travel Matters

Traveling helps me learn new cultures, practice patience, and discover how much more beautiful the world is than any single routine.

Travel is not only a break from everyday life; it is a way to build a more open and creative mindset. Every journey adds a new perspective.

Trip Planning Checklist

A quick guide to make any trip easier, from packing to choosing the best sightseeing plan.

  1. Choose your destination and research local norms.
  2. Book flights and accommodations early.
    • Compare options across several sites.
    • Check cancellation policies carefully.
  3. Prepare a flexible itinerary with rest days.
  4. Pack essentials and leave room for souvenirs.
